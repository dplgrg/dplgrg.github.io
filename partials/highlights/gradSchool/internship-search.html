Cracking the internship search

September 6, 2019 Graduate School

Last year around this time I found myself exploring the bucolic life of rural New England as I began my Masters in Computer Science degree at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. My first year in grad school was a hell of ride. I got to explore entirely new domains, got adjusted to the American way of life, and got the lucky chance to explore some of the best things US had to offer (New York City, I'm looking at you).

But this joyride came with its fair share of stress as along with many other students I was hunting for internships. I interviewed with 10 companies in total, was ghosted by 8 of them and got rejected by Facebook. Needless to say, the pressure was high. February rolled in with good news as I got into one of my dream tech companies to work for - Amazon. I had a great time in Seattle, and I'll be talking about it in my next blog post.
