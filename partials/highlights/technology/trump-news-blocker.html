I made a Chrome extension to block all Trump-related news on Facebook.

July 28, 2018 Technology

Like many millenials, I like wasting time mindlessly scrolling my Facebook news feed. It can serve as a treasure trove of information, but one fine day all I could see was news about Donald Trump. Every second article was about him. It was a huge turn-off so I decided to make a Google Chrome extension to hide all items on the Facebook news feed mentioning Donald or Melania Trump. It took me a total of 2 hours to create it. It currently does a simple patterm match for keywords 'Trump', 'Melania' and 'POTUS' and then hides the div elements entirely from the feed. I am planning to make it intelligent in near future to pick up on recent news articles about Trump-related news. Since I'm not a Twitter user, I didn't include option to remove it from Twitter feed but I am also planning to do so in near future.
